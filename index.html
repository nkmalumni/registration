About Us:
Welcome to our Alumni Registration Portal for the Batch of 2020! Here, you can easily register yourself as an alumni of our institution or browse through the profiles of your fellow classmates.

Our platform is designed to help us keep track of all our alumni, which can be incredibly valuable in various situations, including emergencies. By registering, you'll ensure that we have up-to-date information about you, making it easier for us to stay connected and provide support when needed.

Registering:
To register yourself as an alumni, you have to provide all the required information. Once you submit a registration request, our admin portal will review and verify your information. Once you are verified your registration will be approved and your information will be available on the list.

Note: During the registration process you may be asked for a OTP verification.

Editing Data:
If you wish to edit your data you must go through an OTP verification. Only then you can edit and submit for approval. Like registration, an admin will verify your edited info and approve if eligible.

Accessing Data:
You can only see the alumni information if you are a registered alumni in our platform. Otherwise, you can't access any alumni information.

Deleting Data:
You can request deletation of your data anytime you want. However, OTP verification is a must in that case. Once an admin approves your deletation, your data will be deleted permanently

Terms Conditions and Privacy Policy:
By registering in this platform you agree to share your information with the other alumnis. However, we don't share your data with third parties under any circumstances. Information visible to other alumni are- Name, Nickname, Blood Group, Gmail, Address, College, University.
